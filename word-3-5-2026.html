Word of the Day
Concise
/kənˈsaɪs/
Meaning
Expressing something clearly using only a few words — brief but complete.
Example
"Please write a concise comment in your code so your teammates can understand it quickly."
Malayalam Hint
ചുരുക്കമായി (churukkamaayi) — "Concise" എന്നാൽ കുറഞ്ഞ വാക്കുകൾ ഉപയോഗിച്ച് കൃത്യമായി പറയുക എന്നാണ്.
Tip
Developers love concise writing! Use it in PR descriptions, error messages, and emails: "Keep your commit messages concise." It shows professionalism.
