Word of the Day
Persevere
verb
Pronunciation
pər-sə-VEER
Meaning
To keep trying to do something even when it is difficult or takes a long time.
Example Sentence
"Even when the Laravel deployment failed twice, Mithun decided to persevere and fixed the issue by morning."
Malayalam Hint  🇮🇳
ക്ഷമയോടെ തുടരുക "ബുദ്ധിമുട്ടുകൾ ഉണ്ടായാലും ശ്രമം ഉപേക്ഷിക്കാതെ മുന്നോട്ടു പോകുക" — exactly what you do when debugging code!
Usage Tip
Use persevere for long, ongoing effort — "She persevered through the course." Use persist when something continues (even annoyingly) — "The bug persists." As a developer you'll use both a lot! 😄
