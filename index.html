Clarify
/ˈklær.ɪ.faɪ/
Meaning
To make something easier to understand by explaining it more clearly; to remove confusion or doubt.
Example
"Before starting the project, Mithun emailed the client: 'Could you clarify exactly which features you need by launch day?'"
Malayalam Hint
വ്യക്തമാക്കുക (vyakthamaakkuka) — making something clear, removing confusion. Just like you vyakthamaakkunnu a requirement before writing code!
Usage Tip
Use "Could you clarify…?" in meetings, Slack messages, or emails instead of "I don't understand." It sounds polite and professional. Try: "Could you clarify the expected output?"
