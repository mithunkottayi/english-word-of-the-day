adjective
Diligent
/ˈdɪl.ɪ.dʒənt/
Meaning
Careful, hardworking, and giving full effort and attention to what you do — consistently and without giving up.
Example
"She is a diligent student who reads every chapter twice before the exam."
Malayalam Hint
ശ്രദ്ധാലുവായ, അദ്ധ്വാനശീലമുള്ള — ജോലിയിൽ ശ്രദ്ധയും കഠിനാദ്ധ്വാനവും ഉള്ള ഒരാൾ.
(A person who is careful and hardworking in their work)
Usage Tip
Use diligent to compliment someone's consistent hard work. At your job you might say: "He is a diligent developer — his code is always clean and well-tested."
