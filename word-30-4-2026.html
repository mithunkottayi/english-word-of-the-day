Word of the Day
diligent
/ˈdɪlɪdʒənt/
Meaning
Working hard, carefully, and with steady effort over time — not giving up easily.
Example
"Mithun is a diligent developer — he always reviews his Laravel code thoroughly before pushing to production."
Malayalam Hint
ഉത്സാഹശാലി / ശ്രദ്ധാലു
ഉത്സാഹത്തോടും ജാഗ്രതയോടും കൂടി കഠിനാദ്ധ്വാനം ചെയ്യുന്ന ആൾ.
Think of a student who studies every night without fail — that's diligent.
Usage Tip
Use "diligent" to give a sincere professional compliment.
"She's a diligent worker." or "He made a diligent effort."
It sounds more polished than just saying "hardworking."
