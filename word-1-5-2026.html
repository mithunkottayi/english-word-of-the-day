Persist
/pərˈsɪst/
Meaning
To keep doing something even when it is hard or when others give up.
Example
"Mithun persisted with his English practice every day, and now he speaks with confidence."
Malayalam Hint
തുടരുക / പിന്മാറാതിരിക്കുക
(Thudaruka / Pinmaraathirikuka) — തടസ്സങ്ങൾ വന്നാലും ശ്രമം തുടരുക. Don't stop even when it's difficult.
Tip
Use "persist in" or "persist with" something. In professional emails you can write: "I will persist with resolving this issue." — sounds confident and determined.
