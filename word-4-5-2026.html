Diligent
/ˈdɪl·ɪ·dʒənt/
Meaning
Working carefully and putting in steady, serious effort — someone who does not give up easily and always tries their best.
Example

"Mithun is a diligent developer — he reads every error log carefully and never skips testing before a release."

Malayalam Hint

അധ്വാനശീലൻ / ശ്രദ്ധാലു — ശ്രദ്ധയോടും നിരന്തര പ്രയത്നത്തോടും കൂടി ജോലി ചെയ്യുന്ന ആൾ. "അദ്ദേഹം വളരെ ശ്രദ്ധാലുവായ ജോലിക്കാരനാണ്" എന്ന് പറയുന്നതുപോലെ.

Tip

Use diligent to compliment a teammate: "You've been very diligent with the code reviews." It sounds more professional than just saying "hardworking". Employers love this word in interviews!
